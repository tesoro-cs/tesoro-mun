On April 23rd, 2022, Tesoro High School will be hosting its first-ever regional FLEXMUN Conference which is student-focused, student-led, and focused on issues students want to address. Rather than align so rigidly to traditional components of UN conference structures, we focus on the substance of debate and creativity of resolutions, while emphasizing the unique cultures of the countries each delegate represents.

We invite you to join us as we celebrate another fantastic MUN season and interact with pressing and relevant world issues. To celebrate all of the hard work your delegates have surely undertaken this year, we will end the conference with a small awards and cake ceremony.

This year, Tesoro MUN will also be offering an exclusive Ukraine Crisis, along with Regular committees. More information regarding how these different committees function and what topics are being offered will be released as soon as we obtain a final headcount.

Registration is now open and more information about sign-ups can be found on the “Registration” page of our website.
