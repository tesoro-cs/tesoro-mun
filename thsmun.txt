The Model United Nations club at Tesoro High School has become a very established and recognized Model United Nations organization in Southern California, having won numerous club awards at conferences ranging from the University of California, Irvine, Santa Margarita Catholic High School, Capistrano Valley High School, to other locations. As a Model United Nations delegate, one can expect to develop essential research, debate, public-speaking, organization, and critical-thinking skills, as well as a better awareness of the international community. The delegates from THSNUN have also had other substantial achievements. Our members consistently earn Commendation, Outstanding, Research, and the coveted Gavel (Best Delegate) Awards throughout the year. Members from our club have been accepted and gone on to prestigious learning institutes which include: Harvard, MIT, Duke, Georgetown, Stanford, Cornell, UC Berkeley, UCLA, UCSD, UCI and other schools of higher learning.
